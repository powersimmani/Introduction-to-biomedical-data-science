pH and Biological Systems
pH Scale
Logarithmic scale measuring H⁺ concentration
pH = -log[H⁺]
Range: 0 (acidic) to 14 (basic)
Buffer Systems
Maintain pH stability in blood (pH 7.35-7.45)
Bicarbonate system: H₂CO₃ ⇌ HCO₃⁻ + H⁺
Phosphate and protein buffers
Henderson-Hasselbalch
pH = pKa + log([A⁻]/[HA])
Predicts buffer behavior
Critical for enzyme function
Clinical Relevance
Acidosis (pH < 7.35): respiratory/metabolic
Alkalosis (pH > 7.45): respiratory/metabolic
pH affects protein structure and function
