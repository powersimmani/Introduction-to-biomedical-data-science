Amino Acids Structure
20 Standard Amino Acids
Central α-carbon bonded to:
• Amino group (NH₂)
• Carboxyl group (COOH)
• R group (side chain)
• Hydrogen atom
Classification
Nonpolar: Gly, Ala, Val, Leu, Ile, Met, Pro
Polar: Ser, Thr, Cys, Asn, Gln
Charged (+): Lys, Arg, His
Charged (−): Asp, Glu
Aromatic: Phe, Tyr, Trp
Chirality
All except glycine are chiral
Only L-amino acids in proteins
D-amino acids in bacteria
Ionization States
pKa values determine charge at physiological pH
Zwitterionic form at neutral pH
Critical for protein function
