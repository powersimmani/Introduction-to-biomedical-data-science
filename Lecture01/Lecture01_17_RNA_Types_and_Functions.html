RNA Types and Functions
mRNA
Messenger RNA
• Carries genetic information
• Template for protein synthesis
• 5' UTR, coding sequence, 3' UTR
• Lifespan: minutes to hours
tRNA
Transfer RNA
• Delivers amino acids to ribosome
• 70-90 nucleotides long
• Anticodon-codon matching
• ~60 types in human cells
rRNA
Ribosomal RNA
• Structural/catalytic role
• 80% of cellular RNA
• Forms ribosome core
• Peptidyl transferase activity
Small RNAs
miRNA / siRNA
• Gene regulation (post-transcriptional)
• miRNA: 22 nucleotides
• siRNA: RNA interference
• Therapeutics target
lncRNA
Long Non-coding RNA
• >200 nucleotides
• Gene regulation
• Chromatin remodeling
• X-inactivation (XIST)
circRNA
Circular RNA
• Covalently closed loop
• Highly stable
• miRNA sponges
• Biomarker potential
RNA Therapeutics: mRNA vaccines (COVID-19), siRNA drugs (Patisiran for amyloidosis), antisense oligonucleotides
