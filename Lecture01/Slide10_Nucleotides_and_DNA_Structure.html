Nucleotides and DNA Structure
Nucleotide Components
Base: Purine (A, G) or Pyrimidine (C, T, U)
Sugar: Deoxyribose (DNA) or Ribose (RNA)
Phosphate: Links nucleotides via 5'-3' bond
Watson-Crick Base Pairing
A pairs with T (2 H-bonds)
G pairs with C (3 H-bonds)
Antiparallel strands
Complementarity enables replication
DNA Double Helix
B-form DNA: Most common
Right-handed helix
10.5 bp per turn
Major and minor grooves for protein binding
DNA Parameters
Diameter: ~2 nm
Rise per bp: 0.34 nm
Pitch: 3.4 nm per turn
Stability from base stacking
