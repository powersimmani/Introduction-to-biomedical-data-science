Transcription Process
RNA Polymerase
No primer needed (unlike DNA pol)
Synthesizes RNA 5' → 3'
Uses ribonucleoside triphosphates
Lower fidelity than DNA pol
Promoter Recognition
TATA box (-25 position in eukaryotes)
Transcription factors recruit RNA pol II
Initiation, elongation, termination phases
RNA Processing (Eukaryotes)
5' cap: 7-methylguanosine
3' poly(A) tail: ~200 adenines
Splicing: Remove introns, join exons
Alternative splicing increases diversity
Splicing Machinery
Spliceosome: snRNPs + proteins
Recognizes splice sites (GU...AG)
Branch point formation
One gene → multiple proteins
