Epigenetic Modifications
Epigenetics: Heritable changes in gene expression without DNA sequence alterations
DNA Methylation
• Addition of methyl groups to cytosine
• CpG islands in promoters
• Generally represses transcription
• Maintained through cell division
• Role in X-inactivation
Histone Modifications
• Acetylation (active chromatin)
• Methylation (variable effects)
• Phosphorylation
• Ubiquitination
• "Histone code" hypothesis
Chromatin States
Euchromatin
Open, accessible
Active transcription
Heterochromatin
Condensed, compact
Silenced genes
Disease Implications: Cancer, neurological disorders, metabolic syndromes linked to epigenetic changes
