Structured vs Unstructured Data
Structured Fields
Dropdown selections
Coded diagnoses
Numeric lab values
Easy to query & analyze
Free Text Notes
Clinical narratives
Provider observations
Rich context & nuance
Requires NLP to extract
Hybrid Documents
Many EHR documents contain both structured fields and free-text sections (e.g., radiology reports with findings + impressions)
Images & Signals
Medical imaging (DICOM)
ECG waveforms
Requires specialized processing
Extraction Challenges
Abbreviations & jargon
Negation detection
Temporal reasoning
Key Challenge: ~80% of clinical data is unstructured text
