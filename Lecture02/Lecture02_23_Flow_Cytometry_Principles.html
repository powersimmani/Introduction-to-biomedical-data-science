Flow Cytometry Principles
Fluidics System
Hydrodynamic focusing. Single-cell interrogation at 10,000 cells/s.
Laser Excitation
Multiple lasers: 405, 488, 561, 640 nm common.
Detection Channels
FSC, SSC, multiple fluorescence PMTs. Dichroic mirrors.
Compensation
Correct spectral overlap. Single-color controls essential.
Applications: Immunophenotyping, cell cycle, apoptosis, rare cell detection
