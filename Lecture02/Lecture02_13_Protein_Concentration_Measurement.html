Protein Concentration Measurement
A280 Method
Direct, fast, needs pure protein. ε calculated from Trp/Tyr content.
Bradford Assay
Coomassie dye binding. Sensitive (1-100 μg/mL). Detergent interference.
BCA Assay
Cu²⁺ reduction. Compatible with detergents. 20-2000 μg/mL.
⚠ Interference Factors
Buffer composition critical for accurate measurements.
