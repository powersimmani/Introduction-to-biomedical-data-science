Protein Concentration Measurement
A280 Method
Direct, fast, needs pure protein. ε calculated from Trp/Tyr content.
c = A₂₈₀ / ε
Bradford Assay
Coomassie dye binding. A₅₉₅. Sensitive (1-100 μg/mL). Detergent interference.
BCA Assay
Cu²⁺ reduction. A₅₆₂. Compatible with detergents. 20-2000 μg/mL.
⚠ Interference Factors
DTT, β-ME affect BCA. SDS affects Bradford. Buffer composition critical.
